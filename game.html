AR-Asa's Adventure

This game aim to teach kids(3-8years) about different country’s culture with variety of interactive games, AR 3D models to play with and storytelling (cartoon voice). While working on AR children’s book, it’s very important to think about how a kid will interact with the game application. To make game applications for kids, we need to develop the game with a more attractive, cartoonish look with bright colors and easy to understand UI flow. Also, it’s very important to have a good understanding of 3D models' stylization which keeps kids engaged to the game.
Game Play - Application contains two segments:

1) Augmented Reality - Here 3D augmented characters which come to life when the user places his phone towards the book containing the illustration of the characters in the story. These augmented characters are also given speech functionality to recite the story of that particular section. Kids can even play and interact with the 3D characters

2) Games – In this section, kids can play different games like painting the flag, preparing the food by dropping proper items described in the game, and answer MCQ-based quiz to know how much they learned from the story about the country they visited.

VR-World Runner

• Using the inspiration from Temple Run, Subway Surfer, and Infinity Runner(my old game) an endless runner games , I planned to enhance VR World Runner game using a VR headset to provide an immersive experience as well as encourage the player to perform physical activity.
• Also with my old endless runner game, there was no physical activity involved and the players only used their fingers to play the game. In such kind of games(Temple Run, Subway Surfer), there was no control of the stage.
I kept in mind to improve this game and add all those stuff which were missing in these games(Temple Run, Subway Surfer, and Infinity Runner(my old game)).
Game Play - It is the year 2090. The astronauts from Earth are searching for other planets where life exists.
• MR. CHARACTER (the player); he is on planet surveillance. He flies from Earth and travels to other planets. Before reaching the destination planet, suddenly, his spacecraft trembles and he loses control of the space shuttle.
• The spacecraft crashes over the land, and an extreme wide-angle shot shows the broken spacecraft, its parts are scattered all over the place, and the spaceship is in flames(smoke & dust spreading all over the area).
• As dust spreads out and settles down, we see MR. CHARACTER is standing on the rock, and he is observing the planet. He sees that the whole planet is immersed in lava, and big rocks are floating in the air. MR. CHARACTER hears a voice coming from his radio (another spacecraft is coming for your rescue, but it would be on another side of the planet). A page pops up that shows how a character can play the game and what are the controls player has to use to complete the stage and to reach the spaceship safely.

• When the game starts, it shows a third-person view and camera back of your player:
In VR World Runner, the game objective is to run and control stage blocks by activating and deactivating them, which allows the player to stand or pass through them respectively. Player has to control the blocks by activating/deactivating using a VR VIVE controller and has to jump (in reality) to make the character jump. Furthermore, I have provided two gameplay options in which players can either play infinite levels or they can choose to play stage level, and there are two camera views provided - the third-person and the first-person view

Infinity ∞ Runner

Inspired by some of the superlative, world-class mobile games like Temple Run and Subway Surfer, I developed a similar computer game - ‘Infinity Runner’ with its own unique style and a different gameplay. Character design and the background concept was kept simple but a scintillating color contrast was added to all the levels to make them look attractive. The game comes with an instruction page in the beginning and a pause button during the game.
Game Play - A single player endless running game where the screen and the game’s character are moving continuously and scoring points as it proceeds further. The aim of the game is to reach the end of each level marked by a flag while activating/deactivating the blocks which act as obstacles/void as a part of the gameplay. The player can control the jump of the character and the activation and deactivation of a block.
As levels increase, so does the speed of the character and the difficulty level of the game, making it more exciting for the player. For instance, blocks in the initial stages are stationary but as the levels progress, the blocks also move along with the screen and the character. To make a particular level unpredictable each time it is played, random number generation approach was implemented to number the blocks and change the positions of activated and deactivated blocks. To help the player, two special features were added - 1) Double jump areas, indicated by black blurry screen and 2) Speed-up arrows, on going through which the character speed increases and player can avoid the screen from overtaking the character

Riddler Crisis

Gamification is the application of game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts. Humans learn faster when things are introduced to them by means of games. Today’s popular culture finds many avenues by which complex concepts can be introduced to learners via gaming
This game works on similar lines, with the motive to teach complex graph traversal (Prim’s Traversal Algorithm) concepts to players in a user-friendly manner. Based on the much talked about superhero, Batman’s narrative, and his rivalry with criminal masterminds, this simple game is light-hearted, entertaining and at the same time, a good learning experience.

Game Play - These are dark times. All of the Batman adversaries have returned from their long dormancy. Now, they seek vengeance upon the Dark Knight and are unstoppable by anyone else except you! All of them have captured innocent people and are tormenting them. But Riddler has different plans. As always, he likes to play with his food before eating it. He has carefully placed his suicide bombers at each of the villains' hiding place and intends to detonate if Batman doesn't reach there first. This has formed a network of tasks for Batman. If he fails to reach and defeat the villains in order, the world shall witness death and destruction - APOCALYPSE!!!
Riddler, although evil, is truthful. Seek the path distances from him and bend your mind to crack the riddle and steer your way up. Prepare yourself to face the greatest criminal masterminds of all times.'
